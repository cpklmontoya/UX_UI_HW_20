Department of Labor Website Redesign

The Department of Labor (DOL) website is incredibly bloated and difficult to navigate when searching for something specific.

After redesigning the homepage, the DOL website is much less intimidating and easier to find. The new navigation offers better starting points for finding information.

Initial Testing

I defined the user path that I would use as a baseline for my testing — and subsequently, inform the rest of the project — on what I believed is the reason most people visit the DOL website: unemployment benefits.

Initial user tests revealed a confusing navigation system, roundabout instructions, and links that directed users to other sites.

Through these tests I was able to see what their first instincts were in moving through the website, which offered insight on how best to approach a redesign of the homepage.
